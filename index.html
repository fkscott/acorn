About Us

Established in 1989 Acorn Supply & Distributing, Inc. is an industry leader MBE/WBE/DBE/SBE/SWAM servicing DC, MD, PA, & VA construction material & equipment supplier with an array of NAICS codes to meet all your needs.

Acorn Supply takes pride in providing a seamless experience from the beginning to the end of every project no matter how big or small.
